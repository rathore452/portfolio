FACILITIES

S.D.I.T.S Library

The Institute has a huge and well – maintained
Library with the collection of 27118 books fulfilling
the requirements of various subject of Computer, Electronics,
MCA and allied subjects respectively. The books include
titles ranging from Indian Publications to Foreign ones.
Several periodicals, magazines and
journals form an important part of library. Our library
exercises open access system and the catalogue system is fully computerized
with Lib sys Software. SDITS also has the AICTE approved DELNET
(IEEE Electronic Library) Membership which is available on institute's
intranet. It has two big reading halls, reference section,
magazine section; audio visual section etc.
leading technical journals are readily available. Digital Library
is being planned and system works on e-library.
Institute is having EDUSAT facility.

S.D.I.T.S Infrastructure

The college campus has more than 10 acres of land for its overall development.
The main building of college has majestic attractive look and
covers about 1, 20,000 sqft and of 50,000 sq.mt is in progress.
The building is considered to be one of the finest in the area and
surrounded by nature creating good environments of study.

S.D.I.T.S Seminar Hall

A state of art seminar hall is aesthetically designed where all the important workshops
and seminars are held. The seminar hall is facilitated with the latest technology and
is well – equipped with multimedia projectors. It can accommodate up to 200 students
and interactive sessions are taken up by the teachers.
